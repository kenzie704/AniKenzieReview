The Garden Of Sinners Review!

The garden of sinners isnt an anime that I thought I would enjoy but after watching all 8 movies it has quickly become one of my favourites when watching the show some of the movies were confusing because it felt like some of the later movies should have been closer to the begining of the show. The main character is one of the best out of the series that I have watched, overall it is a really good watch especially if you like anime that focus a bit more on story over action this show follows the comlicated life of shiki ryougi (or ryougi shiki for the way it is said in Japanese).

The Garden Of Sinners Description!

A world were the true dark nature of the human being is being reviewed and exposed by shoking and ruthless events, and in the center of it all we have shiki ryougi, the true heir to the long lived, wealthy and mysterious ryougi clan and mikiya kokutou, a would be normal high school student if it wouldn't be for his amazing deductive and investigative progress.
